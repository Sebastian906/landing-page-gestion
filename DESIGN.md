## Estructura de Diseño y Componentes (Guía de Estilos)

## Brand & Style
The design system is engineered to reflect the intersection of high-end tailoring and industrial-scale manufacturing. The brand personality is authoritative, meticulous, and technologically advanced, targeting C-suite executives and procurement heads in heavy industry and aviation.

The visual style follows a **Corporate / Modern** aesthetic with elements of **Minimalism**. It prioritizes extreme clarity, generous whitespace, and a "technical luxury" feel. The UI should evoke the sensation of a sterile, high-tech textile lab—organized, efficient, and premium. Every element is intentional, avoiding decorative clutter to emphasize the quality of the raw materials and the scale of the manufacturing process.

## Colors
The palette is rooted in traditional corporate power colors, updated with a high-contrast accent to signal technological innovation.

- **Primary (#0A2540):** Used for primary navigation, deep headers, and core brand moments. It represents stability and industrial heritage.
- **Secondary (#1E3A8A):** Used for interactive states and supportive structural elements.
- **Accent (#F59E0B):** Reserved exclusively for high-priority Call-to-Actions (CTAs) and critical status indicators. Its vibrance cuts through the cool blue tones to drive conversion.
- **Neutrals:** Crisp White is the primary canvas. Slate Gray (#475569) is utilized for body text to ensure WCAG AA readability while appearing softer than pure black. Light Gray (#F8FAFC) provides subtle tonal separation for surface backgrounds.

## Typography
The typography system balances the geometric strength of **Sora** for headings with the refined legibility of **Manrope** for body text.

Headlines should be set with tight letter-spacing to appear "locked-in" and architectural. For large display sizes on desktop, use the `display-lg` token to establish immediate hierarchy. All labels use an uppercase transformation with increased letter-spacing to emulate industrial tagging and technical documentation. Body text utilizes `manrope` to maintain a modern, clean line that remains highly readable during long-form procurement reviews.

## Layout & Spacing
This design system utilizes a **Fixed Grid** model for desktop and a **Fluid Grid** for mobile devices. 

- **Desktop:** A 12-column grid with a maximum container width of 1280px. Gutters are fixed at 24px.
- **Mobile:** A 4-column fluid grid with 16px side margins.
- **Rhythm:** Spacing follows an 8px base unit. Use `lg` (48px) and `xl` (80px) vertical spacing between major sections to emphasize the "High Whitespace" brand requirement. This creates a breathing room that suggests luxury and meticulous organization.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth is conveyed through **Tonal Layers** and **Low-contrast Outlines** rather than heavy shadows.

- **Planes:** The base background is White (#FFFFFF). Secondary surfaces like sidebars or content wells use Light Gray (#F8FAFC).
- **Shadows:** Use a single, highly diffused "Ambient Shadow" for elevated cards: `0px 4px 20px rgba(10, 37, 64, 0.05)`. The shadow color is tinted with the Primary Blue to maintain tonal harmony.
- **Borders:** Subtle 1px borders using `#E2E8F0` are used to define boundaries on white backgrounds, providing a crisp, technical edge without visual heaviness.

## Shapes
The shape language is **Soft (0.25rem)**. This provides a precise, engineered feel—sharper than consumer apps but more approachable than raw brutalism. 

- **Standard Elements:** Buttons, inputs, and small cards use a 4px (0.25rem) radius.
- **Large Containers:** Product gallery cards or modal overlays may use `rounded-lg` (8px) to soften the large surface area.
- **Interactive Indicators:** Checkboxes and radio buttons maintain the 4px radius (or full circle for radios) to ensure consistency with the mechanical aesthetic.

## Components
- **Premium Cards:** Feature a 1px border (#E2E8F0) and the signature ambient shadow. On hover, the border color shifts to Steel Blue (#1E3A8A) with a slight 2px upward translation.
- **Buttons:** 
  - *Primary:* Deep Corporate Blue with white text. 
  - *Accent (CTA):* Vibrant Orange (#F59E0B) with dark text for maximum visibility.
  - *Shape:* 4px corner radius, height of 48px for a substantial, tactile feel.
- **Modern Accordion:** Uses a flat style with a subtle background fill (#F8FAFC) for the active header. Icons should be thin-stroke (1.5px) chevron arrows.
- **Timeline:** A sleek 2px vertical line in Steel Blue. Milestones are represented by hollow 8px circles with a 2px stroke, filled with the accent color only for the "current" state.
- **Trust Indicators:** Feature large `display-lg` numbers in Primary Blue. Animate the counter from 0 on scroll-into-view.
- **Product Gallery:** A strict masonry or square grid. Images should have a light gray padding/inset to simulate professional studio photography. Use high-resolution textures of textiles as hover-states.
- **Input Fields:** Use a 1px border with a focus state that adds a 2px Steel Blue outer glow (0.1 opacity). Labels are always positioned above the field in `label-md` style.
